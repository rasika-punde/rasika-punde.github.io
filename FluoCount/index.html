A Fluorescent Bioparticle Counting App

This mobile app brings the power of automated cell counting to your smartphone, making benchtop and smartphone-based fluorescent microscopy even more accessible. Forget expensive software and complex analysis - get accurate bioparticle counts right at your fingertips.

Key Features:
  • Analyze images from both benchtop and smartphone microscopes. One app for all your needs.
  • Fast and accurate results. Efficient image processing delivers reliable counts in no time.
  • Comparable to scientific standards. Get results that match up with the best research software.
  • Easy to use. No need for technical expertise - simply upload your image and let the app do the work.
Whether you're a researcher, clinician, or student, this app is your perfect companion for quick and reliable cell counting. Download it today and experience the future of fluorescent microscopy! Additional keywords: bioluminescence, fluorescence microscopy, cell counting, image analysis, mobile app, point-of-care diagnostics.
