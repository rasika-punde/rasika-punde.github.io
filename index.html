A Fluorescent Bioparticle Counting App

This mobile app brings the power of automated cell counting to your smartphone, making benchtop and smartphone-based fluorescent microscopy even more accessible. Forget expensive software and complex analysis - get accurate bioparticle counts right at your fingertips. Here's what makes this app stand out: - Analyze images from both benchtop and smartphone microscopes. One app for all your needs. - Fast and accurate results. No need for technical expertise - simply upload your image and let the app do the work. Whether you're a researcher, clinician, or student, this app is your perfect companion for quick and reliable cell counting. Download it today and experience the future of fluorescent microscopy!
